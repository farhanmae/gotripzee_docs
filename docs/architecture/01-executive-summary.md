# Executive Summary

## Purpose

This document provides the executive overview of the GoTripzee platform modernization initiative.

## Vision

Build a modern, API-first travel platform where ERPNext serves as the system of record for master data, finance, CRM and operational workflows, while specialized services handle search, booking, pricing, reservations and customer experience.

## Objectives

- Replace tightly coupled legacy components.
- Support B2B and B2C growth.
- Adopt domain-driven design.
- Ensure scalability and maintainability.
- Enable AI-assisted operations.

## Guiding Principles

1. ERPNext remains the source of truth.
2. No core ERPNext modifications.
3. API-first integrations.
4. Event-driven communication.
5. Modular services.
6. Security by design.
7. Observability and automation.

## Expected Outcomes

The target platform will provide improved scalability, extensibility, operational visibility, and faster feature delivery while reducing technical debt.
